Geovanni Rosales Cisneros

GRAPHIC DESIGN

Throughout my childhood, I have always had a knack for creating something out of the blue. It could be as simple as creating a small doodle during a class lecture, or as big as a school project. It wasn't something I figured out could be a potential future, but it was definitely a lesson to learn, as a graphic designer. There are opportunities in each design path you can choose from. I decided to proceed with my path as a graphic designer, to improve my skills and potentially make a career from it, so I can later on support the people who were by my side throughout my life.
