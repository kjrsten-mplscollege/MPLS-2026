Welcome

Please join us in the celebration of diverse and talented designers of The Minneapolis College Class of 2026 Graphic Design and Web Design programs. Despite the obstacles of our current time and place in history these students have chosen to Create and to Design. Their work ranges from skateboard decks to gardening apps and their styles contain everything from retro throwbacks to elements that could only exist today. Thank you for joining us and please enjoy this showcase of fantastic portfolios!
